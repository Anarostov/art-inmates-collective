Prisoner of the Moment Collective

Prisoner of the Moment Collective is a music project built around songs that begin with words. Each release stands on its own, shaped by its own voice, genre, and performer, while remaining tied to the same central idea: the moments that trap us, distort us, or leave something behind.

There is no fixed lineup. Only the song, the voice it requires, and the moment it belongs to.
